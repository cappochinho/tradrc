About

This project was solely authored by myself, Edward Junior Agyemang-Prempeh. It came about when I wanted to automate my cryptocurrency trading activities on Binance. I decided to write my own bot for trading cryptocurrencies after I discovered that trading bots were unreasonably costly and quite complex to use. Tradrc was born out of necessity and will be improved over time as trading algorithms and markets evolve.
